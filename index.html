2. Macro thesis for AI investing in H2 2026

Compute supply

AI infrastructure remains constrained at the advanced accelerator, HBM, CoWoS/advanced-packaging, power and data-center interconnect layers. NVIDIA, TSMC, Broadcom and ASML receive priority because they monetize bottlenecks rather than compete only at the application layer.

Model commoditization

Foundation model quality is converging at the frontier, increasing the importance of distribution, inference cost, safety/regulatory posture, and proprietary workflow data. This favors Microsoft/Azure/OpenAI, Alphabet/Gemini/TPU/search, Amazon/AWS/Bedrock/Trainium, and Anthropic's enterprise-safe positioning.

Application breakouts

Application software can compound, but many names price in agentic adoption before renewal evidence. For first-draft portfolio construction, we prefer mega-cap platforms plus private frontier exposure over expensive vertical apps.

Regulatory backdrop

EU AI Act implementation, US export controls on advanced chips, China/Taiwan geopolitical risk, copyright litigation, safety rules and energy permitting are not footnotes; they are primary underwriting variables.
