# DESIGN

## Brand
- Name: Hermes AI Investment Report — H2 2026
- One-line mission: Turn complex AI market signals into an investor-ready dashboard.

## Colors
- Deep Navy: #071426 — background and research-terminal depth
- Electric Indigo: #533AFD — primary AI/chart energy
- Market Amber: #F4B04A — optimism, highlights, bull character
- Signal Green: #3FBF7F — gains, confirmation, CTA
- Paper Ivory: #F5EFE2 — editorial report text

## Typography
- Display: Georgia / Times-inspired editorial serif for investment gravitas
- UI: Inter / system sans for dashboard labels and ticker chips

## Motion
- Mood: cinematic, playful, polished, 3D-cartoon market lab
- Eases: power3.out for entrances, back.out for character pops, sine.inOut for floating

## Assets
- Final video: assets/videos/ai-dashboard-3d-cartoon-promo.mp4
- Poster: assets/videos/ai-dashboard-3d-cartoon-promo-poster.jpg

## What NOT to Do
- No generic flat SaaS blue gradients.
- No scary bear-market doom visuals.
- No ungrounded investment promises.
- No cluttered small text that fails in a homepage hero embed.
